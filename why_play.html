WHY DO WE PLAY GAMES?
Games teach us many things:
  • To learn to win and lose
     
  • To think and plan
     
  • To improve our motor skills
     
  • To develop our sensory skills
     
  • To count, add and identify colour
     
  • To improve hand-eye co-ordination
     
  • To have fun
