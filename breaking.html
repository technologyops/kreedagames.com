BREAKING NEW PATHS
Kreeda, in pursuit of its philosophy, introduced a new game - Vanavaas. 

Vanavaas is built on mythology and developed into a game through Kreeda’s research, design and innovation. It is not a game rediscovered from the past but one rooted in it. The objective is to create awareness among children of our rich heritage, through fun and play. 

Today fewer children have the joy of listening to stories from their grandparents. Vanavaas could be one way in which the child learns the story. Vanavaas is however, not a substitute for a grandparent, but reinforces the value of one. What better way to play the game than grandparent and grandchild playing together!

Kreeda has broken new paths by creating Vanavaas, in continued pursuit, however, of its basic philosophy. It proposes to pursue this line in creating more games based on heritage and innovation.

Kreeda also announces a number of new initiatives

  • In an effort to keep customers posted about on-going research, Kreeda rules now ask all customers to register by e-mail so a contact can be maintained with them.
  • Kreeda is also working on improving existing games based on customer feedback and suggestions.

At Kreeda we want to remember our games for they tell us a lot about who we are and where we come from. But above all they are a lot of fun.

Kreeda means play in Sanskrit. However here it means a lot more. It means playing in the true spirit with which these games were once played. So ask yourself – Did you Kreeda today?
